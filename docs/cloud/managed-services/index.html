    Overview

    Managed services such as a database, secret storages, or load balancers can be complex to manage on your own. Datapio provides the deployment of various Kubernetes operators in order to handle the complexity.

    The Catalog Operator allows you to enable in your cluster such managed services through ClusterFeature resources. The creation of those resources will trigger the deployment of the corresponding operators to a dedicated namespace.
